Samsung Fridges

In January 1995, Samsung developed a refrigerator featuring an independent fridge and freezer.
This innovation had the benefit of both saving energy and reinforcing the cooling system.
Upon its release in Korea, it became an instant sensation.
With this refrigerator, Samsung ascended to No.
1 in the refrigerator market, and was recognized as the bestselling refrigerator in Korea.,
